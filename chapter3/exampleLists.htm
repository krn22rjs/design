Eggs are one of my favorite foods. Here is a recipe for deliciously rich scrambled eggs.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1tbs butter
  • 2tbs cream

Method

  1. melt butter in a frying pan over a medium heat
  2. Gently mix the eggs and cram in a bowl
  3. Using a patula fold the eggs from the edge of the pan to the center every 20 second (as if you ar making an omelette)
  4. When the eggs are still moist remove from the heat (it will continue to cook on the plate until served
